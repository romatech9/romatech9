## 💻 About ROMA-TECH 🇺🇬

Hi 👋 I'm **ROMA-TECH**, a Ugandan developer, web coder, and WhatsApp bot creator from **Uganda 🇺🇬**.

I was born on **24 November 2005** and grew up in **Kitigoma, Uganda**.

My journey into technology was not easy.

I faced financial challenges and could not continue to university to study coding. But coding was always one of my dreams.

Instead of giving up, I decided to learn through **practice, research, mistakes, testing, and building my own projects**.

At the age of 18, I started building an independent life and looking for work. I worked hard while continuing to learn about technology in my free time.

Today, I continue working and using my free time to explore **coding, web development, Node.js, and WhatsApp bot development**.

> **"It was not easy, but the good thing is — I have it." — ROMA-TECH**

## 🤖 Creator of MUFASER-X 🚀

On **23 May 2026**, I created **MUFASER-X**, a WhatsApp bot project developed by **ROMA-TECH 🇺🇬**.

For me, **MUFASER-X is more than code**.

It represents my dream, my struggle, my learning journey, and my determination to create something of my own.
